Attendance Reporting

You must be marked as attending a class before receiving financial aid for this class. Logging in is not enough to be considered attending the class. In order to have your name submitted as having attended this class, you must:

  1. Introduce yourself to the class on the eLearn Discussion Board by answering the discussion prompt shown there. 
  2. Take the Get Started Here quiz.

I will not submit your name for attendance until you have met the requirements.
