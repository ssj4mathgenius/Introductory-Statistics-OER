How to Get Help

Academic Support

If you are having trouble understanding a topic or concept of this course you should contact me, your instructor, for help.  You can find my contact information on my Instructor Information page.  I can answer questions, suggest resources, study methods, etc.  Please use your course instructors as a resource to help you earn a good grade.
I also encourage you to use these key resources throughout this course:
  • The College Success Zone provides a great list of academic resources that are available to Vol State students.
  • The Thigpen Library is available to help you 24/7 with its online resources (databases) and Research Guides. Use these guides to find the best resources for your class assignment and get help for citations.
  • Tutor.com is available to all Vol State students. It is available 24 hours a day and seven days a week. The link to Tutor.com is part of the eLearn course homepage for each of your classes. The tutoring service covers Math, Business, English, Writing, Science, Health Sciences, Computers, and Foreign Language.
  • The Learning Commons is your one-stop shop for academic support. There is a Learning Commons location on each Vol State campus for math tutoring, test prep, and help with papers and assignments. Visit your location for a list of what’s available and hours.

Questions about the Course

  • Many course questions can be answered by reading the Syllabus or the content in the Get Started Here module. If after reading these documents you still have questions, contact your instructor.
